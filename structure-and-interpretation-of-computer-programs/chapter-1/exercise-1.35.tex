The recurrence relationship is given as:
\[
	\begin{aligned}
		x(n)  & = 1 + \frac{1}{x(n - 1)} \\
	\end{aligned}
\]

For $x(0) = x_0 \in \mathbb{R}$.

As $n \rightarrow \infty$ $x(n)\rightarrow x(n - 1)$.

\[
	\begin{aligned}
		x &= 1 + \frac{1}{x} \\
		x^2 &= x + 1 \\
		x^2 - x - 1 &= 0 \\
	\end{aligned}
\]

\[
	\begin{aligned}
		\Delta &= (-1)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-1) \\
				 &= 5 \\

		    &= \frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2} \\
		x_2 &= \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}
	\end{aligned}
\]

\[
	\begin{aligned}
